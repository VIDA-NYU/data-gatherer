Single cell analysis of human mesenchymal stem cells

Creators

  • Yuchen Gao1
  • Lei Zhang1
  • 1. Institute of Hematology and Blood Diseases Hospital, Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences

Description

This dataset including the RDS files, including meta data, labeled assays, and reduction map information in order to ensure the reproducibility of the scRNA-seq data of human MSCs.

The research "Multi-omics analysis of human mesenchymal stem cells shows cell aging that alters immunomodulatory activity through the downregulation of PD-L1" has been published on Nature Communications (https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-39958-5).
